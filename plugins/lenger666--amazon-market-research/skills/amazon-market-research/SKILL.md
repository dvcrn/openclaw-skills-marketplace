# Amazon Market Research

Generate a **professional Amazon market research report** using a structured **18-step product development framework** widely used by experienced cross-border e-commerce operators.

The report analyzes market size, competition intensity, product opportunities, risks, and profitability potential.

The output is designed for **product selection decisions before development or sourcing**.

---

# What this skill does

This skill performs a **complete product opportunity analysis** and returns:

1️⃣ **Executive summary**

• One-sentence conclusion  
• Market entry recommendation  
• Market opportunity score  
• Recommended price band  
• Key opportunity drivers  
• Core risks  
• Suggested product directions  

2️⃣ **Full professional research report**

The report follows an **18-step Amazon product research framework** used in real product development workflows.
